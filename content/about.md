**Polymux** helps teams build, deploy, and manage intelligent AI agents that automate real workflows—from browser-based tasks to orchestrated multi-agent runs—with observability and guardrails built in.

## What we believe

We think automation should be **reliable**, **inspectable**, and **safe to run in production**. That means clear session boundaries, structured actions, and tooling that helps you understand what an agent did and why—not a black box.

## What we build

- **Agents and orchestration** — Coordinate specialized agents, hand off context, and keep runs deterministic where it matters.
- **Browser automation** — Run repeatable tasks in isolated sessions with human-in-the-loop when you need it.
- **Integrations and credentials** — Connect to your stack with scoped access so secrets stay out of prompts and logs.

## Who we serve

Product, operations, and engineering teams use Polymux to turn repetitive browser and API work into maintained workflows—without sacrificing review, compliance, or rollback paths.
